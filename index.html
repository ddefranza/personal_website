David DeFranza

Ph.D. Candidate, Business Administration (Marketing)

University of Utah


I am a Ph.D. candidate in business administration at the University of Utah. My research is broadly focused on the translation of social science theory to marketing problems using machine learning and econometric methods. My main interest is in examining language as it relates to the perpetuation and influence of consumer stereotypes, cross-cultural marketing, word-of-mouth and social media marketing, branding, and marketing communications.

A brief summary of my experience is provided below. For more details, please download my CV.
